Spring Start Kits

In April 2023, we distributed 40+ "Spring Start" kits to children entering foster care in Gilmer County. Each kit included hygiene products, notebooks, snacks, and a hand-written card to help ease transitions during a difficult time.

Porch Light Packs

As temperatures dropped in Fall 2022, we assembled and delivered “Porch Light Packs” — bags filled with socks, gloves, canned food, and flashlights — to neighbors in rural Ellijay without consistent access to transportation or heating.

Completed
School Notes Drive

In collaboration with a local elementary school, volunteers created bundles of anonymous encouragement notes for kids facing challenges at home. Over 300 personalized notes were delivered before the end of the 2021 school year.

Completed
Back Porch Pantry

In summer 2023, we ran a small community pantry drop-off behind a church in downtown Ellijay. Locals were encouraged to “take what you need, leave what you can,” and over 800 items rotated through the space over two months.
